Excelling in the face of Adversity

As an ITU graduate from the class of 2021, my journey at the University was initially daunting as I encountered something of a culture shock. Surrounded by students from all corners of the country, many of whom were academic toppers in their respective cities, I felt out of place and struggled to establish connections. However, I eventually found my community among middle-class individuals who, like me, sought a balance between enjoying life and being serious about their future.

While facing the challenges of university life, health issues also presented a significant setback when I had to cope with viral Hepatitis, leading to nearly an entire semester of sitting out. As a result, my grades suffered, but upon a full recovery, I returned to the academic arena more determined than ever to succeed. My dedication is evident in my improved performance, as I emerged as one of the top-performing students in the latter half of my degree.

Not shying away from hard work, I embraced a rigorous academic approach, opting to take as many technical subjects as possible and achieving exceptional grades in all of them. Notably, during the final two years of my degree, I even ventured into Masters level courses that intrigued me, demonstrating my passion for learning and my ability to excel in advanced subjects.
