From a young age, my curiosity for technology defied my non-technical upbringing. This curiosity is what led me to start operating a PC on my own since i was 3. By the age 12, i was the dedicated technophile for the whole block that i lived in. From coding small scripts to help with everyday tasks to cracking pirated games, i did it all. Hence, it only made sense for me to choose CS as my major once the time came. Still, it wasn't easy going at first, but i found my footing soon enough and started excelling in all the technical subjects. I even took some classes with Masters students because Bachelors didn't feel challenging enough.
